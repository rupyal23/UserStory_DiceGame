DICE GAME


RULES

Objective of the game is to win all the MONEY before your HEALTH or ENERGY reaches zero.

Each player places a bet on the number of bottles opponent has to drink.

If a player drinks successfully, player WINS the Round by winning the total Money Pot, gains ENERGY but loses HEALTH

If a player loses the bet, in turn loses the ENERGY, loses the MONEY and also loses the HEALTH.

BONUS ! - If people bet the MAXIMUM in a round, winning player will win the pot with the decided Multiplier(2x, 3x or 4x).
